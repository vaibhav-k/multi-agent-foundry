# Corporate Password Policy

## Password Requirements

Corporate passwords must:

- Be at least 12 characters long.
- Include uppercase and lowercase letters.
- Include numbers and special characters.
- Not contain the employee's name or username.

## Password Expiration

Passwords must be updated every 90 days.

## Password Reset

Employees can reset passwords through the self-service identity portal.

If the account is locked after multiple failed attempts, contact the IT Help Desk.
